FAQ

Questions we get.

Is it Verbatim-compatible?

Yes. Import .docx backfiles with highlighting, warrants, and shrinks preserved. Exports open in Verbatim-style templates without reformat.

Does the assistant write cards for me?

No. It searches and ranks cards you've already cut. Warrants come from your evidence — the model is configured to refuse invented claims.

Does it train on my cards?

Your cards index only your workspace. We do not use them to train foundation models. Per-project sharing is explicit and you can turn it off any time.

Does it work on tournament wifi?

Editing works offline. Library sync resumes on reconnect. The assistant falls back to on-device cached embeddings.

What does a squad cost?

$9 per debater per month. First 30 days free. Cancel any time — your cards stay yours.
